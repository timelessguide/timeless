<div class="faq-container">
    <header class="faq-header">
        <h1>Common <em>Questions.</em></h1>
        <p class="subtitle">Everything you might be wondering about the guides and the process.</p>
    </header>

    
    <div class="faq-list">
    <h2>About the Guides</h2>
        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>What is the Core Planning Kit, and who needs it?</h3>
            <p>The Core Planning Kit is the foundational guide for organizing your wishes, documents, and important details. It’s designed for anyone who wants a clear starting point for end-of-life planning.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>What are Additional Guides, and how do I know if I need them?</h3>
            <p>Additional Guides are context-specific resources for situations that need more detailed care — such as caregiving, chosen family, living abroad, or unique responsibilities. Use them only if they apply to your life; the Core Planning Kit is sufficient for most people.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>Can I use the guides at my own pace?</h3>
            <p>Absolutely. These resources are here for when you’re ready. You can start, pause, and revisit sections as your planning needs evolve.</p>
        </div>
        
        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>Is this a legal service?</h3>
            <p>No. Timeless Guides and Timeless Tributes provide organizational tools and reflection prompts. While these can help you prepare for meetings with lawyers, financial advisors, or other professionals, they do <strong>not</strong> replace professional legal or financial advice.</p>
        </div>

    <h2>Buying and Using the Guides</h2>
        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>How are the guides delivered?</h3>
            <p>All guides are hosted on Etsy. After purchase, you’ll receive a secure download link for the PDF files.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>Can I download the guides more than once?</h3>
            <p>Yes! Once purchased, your files remain available through your Etsy account for repeated downloads.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>Can I print the guides?</h3>
            <p>Absolutely. The PDFs are designed to be printable, so you can fill them out digitally or on paper — whichever works best for you.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>Can I skip sections or customize the templates?</h3>
            <p>Yes. Each guide is flexible — you can focus only on what’s relevant to your situation and update your plans over time.</p>
        </div>

    <h2>Inclusivity and Personalization</h2>
        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>My life is different — can I still use these guides?</h3>
            <p>Yes. The Core Planning Kit covers what most lives have in common, while Additional Guides support a wide range of situations, including modern families, LGBTQ+ considerations, people living abroad, or caregiving responsibilities.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>Can someone else help me fill this out?</h3>
            <p>Yes, these guides are designed to be collaborative if that’s helpful. Trusted friends, family members, or professional advisors can work through them with you.</p>
        </div>

    <h2>Practical Reassurance</h2>
        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>What if I don’t understand some of the legal or medical terms?</h3>
            <p>No worries. We focus on the human decisions that matter most. Any technical questions can be saved for your legal or medical professionals.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="faq-item">
            <h3>What happens if I start and then stop?</h3>
            <p>That’s perfectly fine. These guides are flexible — you can return to them whenever you’re ready. Planning at your own pace is encouraged.</p>
        </div>

    </div>
