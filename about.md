            <h2>The Story Behind Timeless Guide</h2>
            <p>I created these resources after seeing how much confusion and administrative weight paperwork can add during already difficult times. Planning shouldn't be a burden when you're grieving.</p>
            
            <p>Timeless Guides is the companion website to <strong>our Etsy store,</strong> where these planning resources are hosted. This site gives you a dedicated space to explore tools, read essays, and plan thoughtfully at your own pace.</p>
            <br/>
            <h3>Why I Made This Kit</h3>

            <p>When my mom was dying, she asked me to write her obituary while she was still alive.</p>
            
            <p>She told me what she wanted it to say — and then reassured me, while I cried, <em>"I'm not dead yet. Don't mourn me while I'm still here. You'll have time for that after."</em></p>

            <p>She was right. When she passed, the obituary was ready. But everything else? It was chaos.</p>

            <p>I remember standing at a government office the day after her death, trying to file papers while carrying my grief. I broke down - because no one tells you how much administration comes with death. No one gives you a map.</p>

            <p>That's why I made this.</p>

            <p>It's not cold or clinical. It's direct, practical, and designed to help you leave clarity behind - not confusion. So the people you care about won't have to choose between falling apart and figuring everything out.</p>

            <p>This project is dedicated to making hard conversations a little easier by providing clear, jargon-free tools that you can use when you're ready.</p>
